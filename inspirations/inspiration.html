these are the 3 artists that inspire me


baz luhrmann

he is is an australian writer, director, and producer best known for his fast and erratic style.
his vision is out of this world; i would rather watch an hour long movie with no words but the most amazing
visuals ever, rather than watch the greatest love story but it will be poorly executed.




zahair murad

zahir is lebanese fashion designer who describes his works as “a glittering world of delicate details
and romantic drama.” he is one of those designers who dresses all of his models like they are crown princesses.




gaylord cuvillier philippe libessart

gaylord is french 2d animator who animates gods' School : the olympian gods.
i found these series by accident and i was so impressed with the story but so upset by the number of
the episodes (too little) then i dug in and discovered that gaylord produces these series himself.
