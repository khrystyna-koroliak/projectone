These are the 3 artists that inspire me

Baz Luhrmann

He is an Australian writer, director, and producer best known for his fast and erratic style. His vision is out of this world; I would rather watch an hour-long movie with no words but the most amazing visuals ever, rather than watch the greatest love story that is poorly executed.

Zahir Murad

Zuhair is a Lebanese fashion designer who describes his works as “a glittering world of delicate details and romantic drama.” He is one of those designers who dresses all of his models like they are crown princesses.

Gaylord Cuvillier Philippe Libessart

Gaylord is a French 2D animator who animates Gods' School: The Olympian Gods. I found this series by accident and was so impressed with the story but upset by the number of episodes (too few). Then, I discovered that Gaylord produces these series himself.
